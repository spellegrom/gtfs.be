GTFS Belgium

GTFS.be offers daily generated GTFS, GTFS-RT and other timetable datasets for whole Belgium. These feeds are made based on the published timetables by the transport agencies, as required by EU 2017/1926. GTFS.be combines, corrects, converts and adds other (public) data. The published feeds are validated, conforms to the standards and can be used directly into your project.

Lets get started »

Services

All our feeds are published free of charge, on a best-effort basis. The most recent version can always be found on this website.

Historical data is available on request. Contact us for more information.

Quality

All our feeds are validated after generation. We're working on fixing issues in the used source data where possible. The feeds can be used directly in existing software, without losing time.

Questions

Issues and discussions related to the feeds are preferably discussed through Github Issues.
